About College :-



  • Vinayakrao Patil Mahavidyalaya has 53 years of history as the flagship of higher education in Vaijapur.

  • Awarded as “College with Potential for Excellence” by University Grants Commission, one of the 149 colleges in India.

  • Re-accredited with “A” Grade by NAAC with CGPA 3.33 (3rd cycle 2016).

  • An ISO 21001-2018 Certified College

  • STRIDE and Paramarsh Schemes by UGC

  • Our College honored as the “Best College Award for NSS” by the Govt. of Maharashtra.

  • Our College honored as the “Ideal Examination Center Award” by the Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad.

  • Study Center of Yeshwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University in B.A., B.Com. & MBA

  • Research Center in Chemistry, Marathi, Hindi & Commerce.

  • Fully computerized Library with over 75,000+ books, research journals, periodicals and rare reference material, comfortable reading halls and Internet facility.

  • Computer Labs, UPS & Generator backup and 40 mbps Internet Leased Line

  • Separate Hostel with comfortable accommodation, Canteen facility at moderate rates.

  • Fully computerised Administrative office.

  • Award winning and achievement oriented NCC & NSS units.
